BIO

I have a wealth of experience in developing commercial and health and social care technical solutions using my IT skills on the Salesforce Platform. I have developed a number of custom concepts for customers ranging from single users to complex multi organisation systems with over 200 users. I am a keen to use and enhance my skills to build solutions and solve problems that benefit the business and the end user. I am able to work well as part of a team. I enjoy taking on projects-based work and I have good communication and presentation skills.

Key Achievements
