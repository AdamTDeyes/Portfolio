What is canvas invader?

Canvas invader is a game, a test and a project that I worked on while at uni. It is built on HTML 5 Canvas, and is to show the capabilities and power of canvas. Below you will find a very simple game, but with very complex background processes. This game will allow you to stress test the performance of Canvas on your system. Feel free to increase the invader amount and watch the FPS change. Originally this bench-marking game was a test between canvas and flash to see which of the two performs better (I'll give you a clue which, its not flash), but now its a bit of history which demonstrates the flexibility of canvas.

The controls are: left/right arrow keys to move, space bar to shoot. If stress testing I would recommend turning on god-mode.
